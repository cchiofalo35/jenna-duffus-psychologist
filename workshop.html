Why This Workshop

Bridging the gap between understanding and change

Many people already have insight into their anxiety. They can recognise the patterns and describe what happens, yet the experience itself often continues.

This workshop was designed to bridge the gap between understanding and change. It brings together psychological science and practical tools in one place, so you can begin working with anxiety in a clearer and more effective way.

What You'll Learn

  • Understand how anxiety functions in the body and brain
  • Recognise why certain loops and thinking patterns repeat
  • See how your nervous system reinforces those cycles
  • Interrupt anxious spirals when they begin
  • Settle your nervous system in the moment
  • Work with thoughts without getting pulled into them
  • Apply these skills in everyday situations

Inside the Workshop

Everything you need in one place

The workshop is delivered through a series of guided audio modules and practical worksheets, designed to help you not just understand anxiety, but begin working with it in real time.

Each module builds on the last, bringing together psychological understanding with practical application so you can start to recognise patterns, respond differently, and integrate these skills into everyday situations.

You can move through the material at your own pace and return to the exercises whenever you need.

Audio Modules

Structured audio modules grounded in psychological science and shaped by clinical work. Designed to be listened to as you move through your day, allowing the material to be absorbed, revisited, and integrated into real life.

Practical Worksheets

Exercises, reflections, and supporting materials designed to help you translate understanding into practice. They're designed to anchor what you're learning so it begins to integrate and hold over time.

Lifetime Access

Move through the material at your own pace and return to it whenever you need. As your awareness deepens, different parts will land differently, giving you something to return to over time.

What This Changes

Coming back to yourself

Recognise anxiety as it's happening before it takes over
The loops that once felt constant loosen and lose their intensity
Feel more able to respond with awareness and choice
Your nervous system becomes something you can work with, not something you feel at the mercy of
Know what to do in the moments that used to overwhelm you
Build self-trust and confidence in yourself
